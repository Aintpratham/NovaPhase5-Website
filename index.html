Good to know

Nova is a tool you own — not a middleman

We want you to buy Nova knowing exactly what it is. A few honest points, so there's never a wrong expectation:

  • Nova isn't a job placement service. It's software. It helps you act quickly on openings Amazon posts — nobody applies on your behalf.
  • Nova doesn't create job openings. Amazon decides when shifts open. Nova makes sure you're among the first to see them.
  • Nova doesn't promise hiring. Hiring decisions always stay with Amazon. Nova gives you the best possible chance to be first in line.
  • Nova never uses your account without you. You sign in on your own computer and stay in control of everything Nova does for you.

FAQ

Common questions

How fast do I get access after paying?

Your license key is emailed automatically right after checkout, usually within minutes. Setup guidance is included, so most customers are up and running the same day.

What exactly am I paying for?

A license for the Nova desktop software, delivered instantly by email. You install it on your own Windows or macOS computer, and it watches for new Amazon warehouse openings for the full length of your plan — with guided setup, updates, and direct support included.

Do I have to give you my Amazon account?

No — never. You sign into your Amazon account yourself, on your own computer, the same way you always do. Your account stays in your hands, and you choose which shifts to apply for.

Is this a subscription?

No. Each plan is a one-time purchase that covers its full duration, with updates included. Nothing renews or charges you again automatically.

Do I need to be technical to set it up?

Not at all. Setup is a short, guided process that takes a few minutes on Windows or macOS, and direct support is there if anything is unclear.

Will Nova get me a shift?

Nova removes the slowest part of the process — noticing that an opening exists so you can go after it. Which shifts appear still depends on your location and timing, and hiring is always Amazon's decision, so results vary from person to person.
